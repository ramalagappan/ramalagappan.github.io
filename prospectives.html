Prospective PhD Students


I am currently looking for motivated graduate students to work with me. If you are not yet admitted to UIUC, drop me a note with the following: 1. your CV, 2. a little bit about your prior systems-building experiences, and 3. your thoughts about one of our recent papers. Additionally, I will be very interested if you have something to say about how to extend/improve our prior work. I will contact you if there is a good fit.

If you are already a graduate student at UIUC, you are welcome to schedule a meeting with me here.

My research interests are broadly in the area of systems, with a particular focus on storage and distributed systems. I am looking for students to work on the following topics:

  • Distributed storage reliability - See PAR, Redundancy, Correlated
  • Replicated storage performance- See Nilext
  • ML for systems - See Bourbon
However, I am open to exploring new areas, especially if you have prior research experience in that area.
